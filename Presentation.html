EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS

Key Findings from EDA

Class Imbalance
88.7%
Majority class "no subscription". Accuracy alone is meaningless — baseline always predicting "no" scores 88.7%.
Duration Leakage
3–5×
Subscribers stay on calls 3–5× longer. Post-hoc feature — unknown at prediction time. Classic data leakage.
Economic Signal
Euribor
Euribor 3m rate correlates with subscription rates. Employment rate and CPI also show significant trends.
Key Categories
poutcome
Prior campaign success is the strongest categorical predictor. Cellular > telephone. Education and job matter.

BONUS: EXTERNAL DATA FROM BANCO DE PORTUGAL

Macro-Economic Context

5 Portuguese economic indicators
emp.var.rate, cons.price.idx, cons.conf.idx, euribor3m, nr.employed — sourced from Banco de Portugal, reflecting the 2008–2010 financial crisis recovery.
Euribor 3m: strongest inverse correlation
When interest rates fall, term deposits become less attractive — yet subscription rates rose during the crisis period (counterintuitive recovery signal).
Multicollinearity present
euribor3m ↔ nr.employed: ρ = 0.94. Tree-based models handle naturally; Logistic Regression needs regularization.
Decile scatter analysis confirms: economic features demonstrably improve prediction — all 5 rank in top-20 importance.
FEATURE ENGINEERING
06 / 13
4 COMPOSITE FEATURES

Engineering Predictive Signal

01 - BINARY FLAG
pdays_was_contacted
pdays ≠ 999 → 1
Decouples the 999 sentinel from the numeric scale. pdays=999 means "never contacted" — a categorical distinction, not a measurement.
02 - RFM-INSPIRED
engagement_score
(previous+1) / (campaign+1)
How engaged is the client relative to contact frequency? Higher = proven contact history with fewer touches needed.
03 - INTERACTION
loan_burden
housing_bin + loan_bin
Total active loans (0, 1, or 2). Captures the combined financial pressure from housing + personal loans.
04 - MACRO PROXY
euribor_cpi_spread
euribor3m − CPI/100
Real interest rate proxy. Positive spread = term deposits more economically attractive relative to inflation.
ALL 4 IN TOP-20 Each engineered feature appears in top-20 importance rankings — confirming domain-driven feature engineering adds real signal.
PREPROCESSING PIPELINE
07 / 13
5 STEPS - 53 FEATURES AFTER ENCODING

From Raw Data to Training-Ready

STEP 01
"Unknown" as Category
Preserve unknown strings in job, education, contact — carries predictive signal, not missing at random.
STEP 02
Binary + One-Hot Encode
Manual 0/1 for default, housing, loan. OneHotEncoder (drop='first') for 7 multi-category features.
STEP 03
Feature Engineering
4 composite features: pdays_was_contacted, engagement_score, loan_burden, euribor_cpi_spread.
STEP 04
StandardScaler + SMOTE
Scale 13 numerical (base+econ+engineered). SMOTE → 58,476 balanced samples. No class_weight.
STEP 05
80/20 Stratified Split
32,950 train / 8,238 test preserving class ratio. Duration excluded — production-realistic.
53 features total: 37 one-hot + 13 scaled numerical + 3 binary passthrough - random_state=42 for reproducibility

CHALLENGES & LESSONS
11 / 13
WHAT WE LEARNED

Challenges & Solutions

CORE LESSON
The best model deploys — not the one with the highest AUC.
Removing duration drops AUC from 0.95 to 0.80 — but makes the model production-viable.
01
Class Imbalance (11.3%)
Accuracy is deceptive at 88.7%. SMOTE + F1/Precision/Recall metrics + lift analysis.
02
Data Leakage (Duration)
Strongest predictor is post-hoc. Two models: production (AUC 0.80) vs reference-with-leakage (AUC 0.95).
03
Economic Multicollinearity
euribor3m ↔ nr.employed: ρ = 0.94. Domain shift risk: 2008–2010 crisis data.
04
Double Correction Trap
SMOTE + class_weight over-corrects. Single-method balancing yields better results.
BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
12 / 13
8 ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS

Improving Campaign ROI

01 - TARGETING
Prioritize poutcome = success
Previous campaign success is consistently the #1 predictor. These clients are proven responders.
02 - CHANNEL
Prefer cellular contact
Cellular outperforms landline telephone. Second-ranked categorical feature by importance.
03 - MACRO
Time campaigns to euribor_cpi_spread
When real interest rates are positive (Euribor > CPI), term deposits become more attractive.
04 - ENGAGEMENT
Target high engagement_score
High ratio of previous to current contacts signals receptiveness — top-20 engineered feature.
05 - FREQUENCY
Limit campaign contacts
High campaign count correlates with lower conversion — diminishing returns, potential annoyance.
06 - FINANCIAL
Target zero loan burden
loan_burden = 0 consistently associates with higher subscription probability across all models.
07 - DEPLOY
XGBoost lead scoring API
Rank contacts by probability, target top deciles, tune threshold (0.31 optimal at 4:1 cost ratio).
08 - MONITOR
Watch economic drift
Model trained on 2008–10 crisis data. Update economic indicators and recalibrate for current regime.
